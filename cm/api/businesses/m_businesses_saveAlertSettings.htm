PUT /api/businesses/{BusinessID}/alertsettings

Updates the configurable alert settings for a business on the platform.

Note: When you change a setting, it might take up to five minutes for the change to take effect.

Authorization Roles/Permissions: Must be logged in. Must have Modify permission for the resource. Site Admin.

Item Value
