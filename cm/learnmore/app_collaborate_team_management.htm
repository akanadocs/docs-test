What are the components of the Team page?

My Apps includes a Team page that allows you to create your application development team by sending out invitations to members. Team members can then be viewed and managed in a summary listing.

Invite Team Members

The Invite More function allows you to send invitations to individuals that you would like to be part of your application development team.

  • Existing team members are displayed in a summary listing.
  • When an invitation is sent a "Team Invite" request email is issued.
  • The invitee receives an invite on their Dashboard page. If the invitee is not a member, they must sign up using the same email address that the invitation is addressed to in order to accept the invitation via their Dashboard.
  • While an invitation is still pending, a Resend Email button is available so you can remind the invitee.
  • At any point after a member has been invited, a Remove button is available so that you can remove the invitee or team member.
  • See How do I create a development team?

View Team Members

When a team member invitation is submitted, a new entry is added to the list with the user name, email address, and an initial invitation status of Pending.

Manage Team Members

Team members can remove a Team Member from the listing.

How do I create a development team?

The My Apps > Team page includes a team member invitation function that allows you to send out invitations to individuals that you would like to be part of your application development team.

  • When you create an app, you are the first team member.
  • Your team membership is automatically approved and is posted to the Team Summary.
  • You can invite additional team members using the Invite More function.
To create a development team:
  1. Navigate to My Apps > App Name > Team. The Team page displays.
  2. Click Invite More. The Invite Team Members page displays.
  3. In the Email text box, enter the email address of one or more individuals you would like to invite to your development team. If you're inviting more than one person, separate email addresses with a comma.
  4. In the Add a Brief Message text box, specify a personal message that will be sent to the people you're inviting.
  5. Click Invite. The invitation email is sent. The platform also posts an invitation notification to the member's Dashboard. The invited member can then log into the platform to accept or decline the Team invitation.

Note: When you create an app, you are the first team member. You can then invite additional team members using the Invite More function. Invitees that are not members must sign up for the platform before accepting the team invitation.

How do I respond to an app team invitation?

If you receive an invitation via email to join an app development team on the platform, you can accept or reject the invitation.

Note: You must be a platform member to join a development team. If you have already received a development team invitation and are not currently a member, you must sign up using the email address that the team invitation was issued to in order to accept the invitation. If you are not currently a member, see Basics for more information.

To accept a team invitation:
  1. Log in to the platform (you can click the link in the invitation email) and go to your Dashboard page.
  2. Find the team invitation.
  3. Click Accept. Enter a comment if you want to, and then click Confirm.

To view information about the app team, click My Apps, choose an app, and then click Team. The Team page displays, with a summary of information for each team member, including user name, email address, and invitation status.

To decline a team invitation:
  1. Log in to the platform (you can click the link in the invitation email) and go to your Dashboard page.
  2. Find the team invitation.
  3. Click Decline. Enter a comment if you want to, and then click Confirm.

How do I remove a team member from my development team?

Any team member can remove team members from their development team.

To remove a team member from an app development team:
  1. Navigate to My Apps > App Name > Team. The Team page displays.
  2. To remove a team member from your development team, target the line item of the team member and click Remove.

How do I cancel a team invitation?

Once a team invitation is sent, the invitation itself cannot be revoked. A member can choose to accept or decline the invitation. If they choose to accept the invitation, you can remove the team member from your development team on the My Apps > Team page by clicking Remove. See How do I remove a team member from my development team?
