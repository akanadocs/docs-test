Business Administrator Invited, to Invited Member

Values

Notification Type
com.soa.notification.type.bizadmin.member.invited.invitee
Trigger
A business administrator invites someone else to be an administrator for the same business
Recipients (1)
Only the person invited (existing administrators for the same business receive a different notification)
Notification Category
None.
Dashboard Subject
{business.name}
Dashboard Content
{author.username} has invited you to be a business administrator for {tenant.name}
Email Subject
You''ve been invited to be a business administrator for {tenant.name}
Email Body
<p>Hello from {tenant.name},</p>
<p>{invitingmember.username} has invited you to be a business administrator for a business on {tenant.name}.</p>
<p>Here''s the message {invitingmember.username} wrote to you:</p>
<p>"{invitation.text}"</p>
<p>If you already have a {tenant.name} account, click {tenant.theme.console.address} to accept or reject your invitation. If you''re not a member yet, please sign up at {tenant.theme.console.address}#/home/signup/{invitation.code} using your {invitedmember.email} email address.</p>
<p>Thanks,<br />
The {tenant.name} Team</p>
{system.email.footer.html}

Business Administrator Invited, to Business Team

Values

Notification Type

Trigger
A business administrator invites someone else to be an administrator for the same business
Recipients (1+)
Existing administrators for the same business (invited person receives a different notification)
Notification Category
None.
Dashboard Subject
{business.name}
 
Dashboard Content
{invitedmember.name} has been invited to be a business administrator for {tenant.name}
Email Subject
{invitedmember.name} has been invited to be a business administrator for {tenant.name}
Email Body
<p>Hi Business Administrator,</p>
<p>A new member has been invited to accept  a Business Administrator role for a business on {tenant.name}. Please refer to the details below:</p>

<p>Thanks,<br />
The {tenant.name} Team</p>

Business Team: Invitation Resent, to Invited Member

Values

Notification Type
com.soa.notification.type.bizadmin.member.inviteresent.invitee
Trigger
A business administrator resends an invitation to be an administrator for the same business
Recipients (1)
Only the person invited. Could be a platform member or non-member.
Notification Category
None.
Dashboard Subject
{business.name}
 
Dashboard Content
{author.username} has resent your invitation to be a business administrator
Email Subject
Reminder: You''ve been invited to be a business administrator for {tenant.name}
Email Body
<p>Hello from {tenant.name},</p>
<p>This is a reminder that {invitingmember.username} has invited you to be a business administrator for a business on {tenant.name}. Please follow the instructions below to accept or reject the invitation.</p>
<p>Here''s the message {invitingmember.username} wrote to you:</p>
<p>"{invitation.text}"</p>
<p>If you already have a {tenant.name} account, click here {tenant.theme.console.address} to accept or reject your invitation. If you''re not a member yet, please sign up at {tenant.theme.console.address}#/home/signup/{invitation.code} using your {invitedmember.email} email address.</p>
<p>Thanks,<br />
The {tenant.name} Team</p>

User Deleted: To Business Admins

Values

Notification Type
com.soa.notification.type.bizadmin.userdeleted
Trigger
Site Admin removes a user.
Recipients (1+)
All business admins
Notification Category
None.
Dashboard Subject
User Removed
 
Dashboard Content
{user.name} is no longer a user on {tenant.name}
Email Subject
User removed ({user.name})
Email Body
<p>Hello,</p>
<p>A user, {user.name}, has been removed by a Site Administrator, {author.username}.</p>
<p><strong> Note:</strong> The user might have been registered but could also have been Pending Validation (registration process incomplete) or Invited (user was invited but didn't yet accept). If the user was registered and added content, the content was not deleted.</p>
<p>If you have questions or need help, feel free to contact us at <a href="mailto:{tenant.contact.email}">{tenant.contact.email}</a>.</p>
<p>Thanks,<br />
The {tenant.name} Team</p>
{system.email.footer.html}
