  • Asymmetric Signature: http://www.w3.org/2000/09/xmldsig#rsa-sha1
  • Symmetric Signature: http://www.w3.org/2000/09/xmldsig#hmac-sha1
  • Digest Algorithm: http://www.w3.org/2000/09/xmldsig#sha1
  • Encryption Algorithm: http://www.w3.org/2001/04/xmlenc#aes256-cbc

  • Asymmetric Key Encryption Algorithm: http://www.w3.org/2001/04/xmlenc#rsa-oaep-mgf1p

  • Signature Key Derivation: http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/ws/2005/02/sc/dk/p_sha1
