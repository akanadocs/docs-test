Supported Platforms: 7.0 and greater.
To view binding details
  1. Log in to the Policy Manager Management Console.
  2. Go to Configure > Registry > Bindings.
  3. On the Bindings Summary screen, select a binding line item and select View Binding Details from the Actions drop-down list. The Binding Details screen is displays.
