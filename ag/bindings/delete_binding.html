Supported Platforms: 7.0 and later.
To delete a binding
  1. Log in to the Policy Manager Management Console.
  2. Go to Configure > Registry > Bindings.
  3. On the Bindings Summary screen, click View Binding Details.
  4. Select the binding you want to delete.
  5. From the Actions drop-down list, select Delete Binding.

    Note: Only bindings that contain zero references display the delete function. If a binding is referenced, you must remove the references before deleting the binding.

  6. Click OK.
