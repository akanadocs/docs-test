Supported Platforms: 7.0 and greater.
To view binding service references
  1. Log in to the Policy Manager Management Console.
  2. Go to Configure > Registry > Bindings.
  3. On the Bindings Summary screen click View Binding Details.
  4. Target the name of the binding that you would like to view service references for.
  5. From the Actions drop-down list, select View Service References.
    The Workbench > Search screen displays showing the references associated with the selected binding in the "Number of References" column.
    Note: The "Number of References" column shows both references from bindings and services.
